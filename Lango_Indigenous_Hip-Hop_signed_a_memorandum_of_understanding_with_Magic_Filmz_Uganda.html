Lango Indigenous Hip-Hop signed a memorandum of understanding with Magic Filmz Uganda

Lango Indigenous Hip-Hop is delighted to sign an MoU with Magic Filmz Uganda on a joint project that is utilising the power of the Arts and culture to tackle the problems faced by people living with HIV/AIDS.

Magic filmz Uganda is a youth-led filmmaking company that is based in Northern Uganda with several movie products attached to its name. This project not only brings a movie product for our communities, but also engages youth in many ways, thus contributing to your vision “To build a powerful youth generation for that is not limited by imagination or self-expression, rising to make positive difference our global society.
